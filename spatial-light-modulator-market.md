<p><p>The Spatial Light Modulator (SLM) market can be categorized into two types: Reflective SLM and Transmissive SLM. Reflective SLMs use reflective materials to manipulate light. They modulate light intensity or phase by reflecting incident light waves. Transmissive SLMs, on the other hand, use transparent materials and control light by modulating its transmission properties. Both types find applications in diverse fields such as holography, laser beam steering, adaptive optics, and optical communication systems. These SLM technologies play a crucial role in enhancing display resolution, optical data processing, and imaging quality.</p></p>
