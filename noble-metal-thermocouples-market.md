R Type thermocouples are made of platinum and rhodium and are generally used in high-temperature applications. S Type thermocouples are made of platinum and rhodium and are mainly used in the industrial sector. B Type thermocouples are made of platinum and rhodium and are commonly used in lower temperature applications.</p></p>
