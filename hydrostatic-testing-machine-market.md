<p><p>A hydrostatic testing machine is a device used to test the strength and integrity of pressure vessels such as pipes, cylinders, and tanks. The market is driven by factors such as increasing demand for pressure vessels in various industries, stringent safety regulations, and the need for regular inspection and maintenance of existing infrastructure.
