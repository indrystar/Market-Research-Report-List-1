<p><p>The semiconductor processing heater market consists of various types, namely Immersion, Pedestal, Platen, and Others. Immersion heaters are designed to be immersed directly into the liquid or chemical being processed. Pedestal heaters are mounted on a support, providing uniform heat distribution. Platen heaters are flat, rectangular plates used for uniform heating of wafers or substrates. The "Others" category includes various types of heaters that do not fall into the aforementioned categories, such as quartz tube heaters or strip heaters. Each type caters to specific heating requirements in semiconductor processing.</p></p>
