There are two main types of automotive side airbags: side torso airbags and side tubular or curtain airbags. Side torso airbags are typically located in the seat and are designed to protect the chest and abdomen of the occupant. Side tubular or curtain airbags deploy from the roof or side frame of the vehicle to provide protection for the head and neck in the event of a side-impact collision.</p></p>
