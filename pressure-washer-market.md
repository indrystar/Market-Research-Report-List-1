<p><p>Pressure washers are categorized into three main types based on their power source: electric motor, petrol engine, and diesel engine. Electric motor pressure washers are ideal for smaller tasks and indoor use as they are quiet, lightweight, and require less maintenance. Petrol engine washers are more powerful and suitable for outdoor tasks, making them popular among homeowners and professionals alike. Diesel engine washers are the most powerful option, mainly used for heavy-duty commercial and industrial applications that require high pressure and extensive cleaning power.</p></p>
