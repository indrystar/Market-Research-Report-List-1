<p><p>The Rubber Moulding Hydraulic Press market includes two main types of machines, namely the Vacuum Type Rubber Compression Molding Machine and the Rubber Compression Molding Machine (Column Type). The Vacuum Type machine utilizes a vacuum to remove air from the mould cavity, allowing for more precise and efficient rubber compression. On the other hand, the Rubber Compression Molding Machine (Column Type) uses columns to provide support during the rubber compression process. Both machines are essential in the rubber moulding industry, offering different advantages and applications for various manufacturing needs.</p></p>
