<p><p>The retail point-of-sale (POS) system market consists of two main types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based POS systems store data on remote servers accessed through the internet while web-based systems store data locally but require internet connectivity to access. Cloud-based systems offer convenience and scalability as they can be accessed from anywhere with an internet connection, making them suitable for businesses of all sizes. Web-based systems, on the other hand, are generally less expensive and provide more control over data security, making them popular among smaller businesses.</p></p>
