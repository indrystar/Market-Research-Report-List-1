<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Identity Verification Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Verification</li><li>Dynamic Verification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Identity verification software is categorized into two types: static verification and dynamic verification. Static verification involves matching the personal information provided by an individual against pre-existing databases. It verifies the identity at a fixed point in time and is often used in document verification processes. On the other hand, dynamic verification constantly monitors and verifies an individual's identity throughout their online activities, utilizing various data points, behavioral biometrics, and artificial intelligence algorithms to ensure continuous authentication and protection against fraud. Dynamic verification offers a higher level of security compared to static verification.</p></p>
