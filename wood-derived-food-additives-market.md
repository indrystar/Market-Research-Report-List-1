Lignin is utilized as an emulsifier in food processing. Xylan is a type of hemicellulose used to improve texture and moisture retention in foods. Vanillin, derived from wood pulp, is a popular flavoring agent in bakery and confectionery products. These wood-derived additives offer natural, sustainable alternatives to synthetic additives in the food industry.</p></p>
