<p><p>The SaaS-based CRM software market includes two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based CRM software is delivered over the internet, allowing users to access it remotely through web browsers. It offers flexible scalability, automatic updates, and reduced maintenance costs. On the other hand, on-premises CRM software is installed directly on company servers and managed internally. It provides more control over data and customization options but requires higher initial investment, on-site maintenance, and upgrades. Both types cater to different customer preferences and business needs in the CRM software market.</p></p>
