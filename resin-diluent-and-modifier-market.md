<p><p>Resin Diluent and Modifier market comprises of two main types: Non-reactive Type and Reactive Type. Non-reactive Type includes diluents that do not chemically react with the resin, helping to reduce viscosity and improve workability. On the other hand, Reactive Type modifiers chemically interact with the resin, altering its properties and enhancing performance characteristics. Both types play vital roles in adjusting viscosity, improving adhesion, and increasing flexibility in various applications such as coatings, adhesives, and composites.</p></p>
