<p><p>FHP AC Motors refer to small-sized electric motors used in various applications. Split phase motors are commonly used in home appliances and have two windings, enabling them to start by creating a rotating magnetic field. Shaded pole motors are designed with a shading coil to create a rotating magnetic field, making them suitable for low-power applications like fans or pumps. Reluctance motors operate based on the principle of magnetic reluctance and are used in appliances requiring high starting torque, such as air conditioners or refrigerators.</p></p>
