<p><p>The meat deboning machine market is divided into two types: fully automatic deboning machines and semi-automatic deboning machines. Fully automatic deboning machines are designed to completely automate the deboning process, with minimal human intervention required. They can handle large volumes of meat and are generally more efficient and faster. On the other hand, semi-automatic deboning machines are partially automated and require some manual labor. They are suitable for smaller-scale operations and offer a balance between automation and human involvement.</p></p>
