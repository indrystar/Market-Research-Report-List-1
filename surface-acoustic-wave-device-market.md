<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) devices are used for various applications in the electronics industry. Filters are SAW devices that selectively attenuate or amplify specific frequencies, ensuring signal clarity. Oscillators generate stable, high-frequency signals for devices like radios and smartphones. Resonators enhance the performance of filters and oscillators by improving their frequency stability. Transducers convert electrical signals into acoustic waves and vice versa, enabling communication between electronic devices and the physical world. "Others" refer to SAW devices used in diverse applications such as sensors, delay lines, and wireless communication systems.</p></p>
