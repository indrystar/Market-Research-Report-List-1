<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multistage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The backpressure steam turbine market is divided into two main types: single-stage and multistage. </p><p>A single-stage turbine operates with one set of nozzles and blades, converting the pressure of steam into rotational energy in a single step. This type of turbine is typically used in applications where lower pressure steam is available for power generation.</p><p>On the other hand, a multistage turbine contains multiple sets of nozzles and blades arranged in series. Each stage extracts additional energy from the steam, resulting in higher overall efficiency. Multistage turbines are commonly used in power plants and industrial processes where higher pressure steam is available.</p></p>
