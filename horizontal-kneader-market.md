<p><p>A Horizontal Kneader is a type of mixing equipment used in various industries. It can be categorized into dynamic and static markets based on its working principle and design. In the dynamic market, the kneader uses mechanical force, such as rotating blades, to create a shearing motion for mixing. On the other hand, the static market kneader relies on static mixing elements, such as baffles or stationary blades, to achieve mixing without any mechanical moving parts. Both types offer unique advantages and applications depending on the desired mixing process.</p></p>
