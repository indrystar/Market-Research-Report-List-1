<p><strong>The Industrial Infrared Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Camera</li><li>Stereo Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Infrared Camera Market has two main types: Standard Camera and Stereo Camera. Standard Cameras are traditional infrared cameras that capture thermal images for industrial applications. They provide a single perspective view and are cost-effective. On the other hand, Stereo Cameras use two lenses to capture thermal images, enabling depth perception and more accurate measurements. They are suitable for applications that require precise analysis of depth, such as 3D imaging, robotics, and autonomous vehicles. Both types serve different purposes and cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
