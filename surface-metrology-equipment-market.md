<p><strong>The Surface Metrology Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface metrology equipment is used to measure and analyze the surface characteristics of materials. Contact surface metrology equipment involves direct contact with the surface being measured. This includes probes, stylus instruments, and profilometers. On the other hand, non-contact surface metrology equipment uses technologies such as optical and laser systems to measure surfaces without physically touching them. This includes instruments like interferometers and confocal microscopy. Both contact and non-contact metrology equipment have their advantages and applications and are used in various industries for quality control and research purposes.</p></p>
