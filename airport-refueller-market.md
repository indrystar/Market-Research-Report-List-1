<p><p>The airport refueller market can be classified into three main types - self-propelled, towed, and others. Self-propelled refuellers refer to vehicles that have their own engines and can be driven independently. Towed refuellers are pulled by another vehicle and do not have their own propulsion. The "others" category includes alternative refuelling methods such as hydrant dispensers or refuelling trucks that operate outside the traditional self-propelled or towed models. These different types offer varying levels of mobility, efficiency, and capacity to cater to the diverse needs of airports and airlines.</p></p>
