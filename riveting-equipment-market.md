<p><p>Riveting equipment is used to fasten two or more objects by deforming their materials. There are three main types of riveting equipment in the market: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Pneumatic riveting equipment utilizes compressed air to deliver the required force for the riveting process. Hydraulic riveting equipment operates through the use of fluid power, allowing for higher force and precision. Electric riveting equipment incorporates electric motors to generate the necessary force. These different types of riveting equipment provide various options for manufacturers to choose from based on their specific needs and operational requirements.</p></p>
