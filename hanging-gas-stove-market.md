<p><p>In the hanging gas stove market, there are two main types: condensing and non-condensing. Condensing gas stoves are designed to efficiently use heat by extracting and reusing waste gases, resulting in lower energy consumption and reduced emissions. They are more environmentally friendly and cost-effective in the long run. Non-condensing gas stoves, on the other hand, do not recycle waste gases and have a simpler design. While they may be cheaper upfront, they tend to be less efficient and may have higher energy costs over time.</p></p>
