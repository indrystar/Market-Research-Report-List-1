<p><p>A pin header, also known as a male header or male connector, is an electronic component that consists of a row of pins on a plastic strip. The pins on the header are designed to be inserted into a socket or a female header, creating a reliable electrical connection.</p><p>The pin header market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. This can be attributed to the increasing demand for pin headers in various applications such as automotive, consumer electronics, industrial automation, and telecommunications. The automotive sector, in particular, is driving the market growth with the increasing adoption of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and infotainment systems.</p><p>Additionally, the growing popularity of IoT (Internet of Things) devices is also fueling the demand for pin headers. These devices require reliable interconnection solutions to enable communication between various sensors and components. Furthermore, the rising demand for compact and high-density electronic devices is boosting the market growth as pin headers provide a space-saving solution for PCB interconnections.</p><p>In terms of trends, miniaturization is becoming a key focus area for pin header manufacturers.
