<p><p>The Mobile Substation market can be categorized into two main types: HV (High Voltage) Mobile Substations and LV/MV (Low Voltage/Medium Voltage) Mobile Substations. HV Mobile Substations are designed to handle high voltage power distribution systems, typically operating at levels above 100 kV. They are used in situations where quick and temporary power supply is required. On the other hand, LV/MV Mobile Substations are used for low voltage and medium voltage power distribution systems, typically operating at levels below 100 kV. They are more commonly utilized in situations where temporary power supply is needed for construction sites, events, or emergencies.</p></p>
