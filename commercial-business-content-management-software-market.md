<p><p>Commercial Business Content Management Software is categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise software is installed on local servers and managed within an organization's environment. This offers higher control and customization but requires significant hardware and maintenance costs. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is hosted on the internet and accessed via web browsers. It provides flexibility, scalability, and reduces IT burden as all maintenance and updates are handled by the software provider. Cloud-Based software is subscription-based with data stored securely in the cloud.</p></p>
