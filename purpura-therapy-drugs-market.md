<p><p>Purpura therapy drugs market includes treatments for two main types of purpura: Allergic Purpura and Thrombocytopenic Purpura. Allergic Purpura is characterized by inflammation of blood vessels, while Thrombocytopenic Purpura is a condition where the immune system destroys platelets, leading to excessive bleeding. Both types require specific medications such as corticosteroids, immunosuppressants, and intravenous immunoglobulins to manage symptoms and prevent complications. The market for these drugs is driven by the increasing prevalence of these conditions and the need for effective treatments.</p></p>
