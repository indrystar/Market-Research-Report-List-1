<p><p>Electrolyte drinks are classified into two types: natural and artificial. Natural electrolyte drinks are made from ingredients derived from natural sources, such as fruits, vegetables, and herbs. These products are marketed as healthier options, as they contain no artificial additives or preservatives. Artificial electrolyte drinks, on the other hand, are formulated using synthetic ingredients and chemical additives to mimic the electrolytes found naturally in the body. These products are often marketed as convenient and readily available alternatives to natural sources of electrolytes, especially for athletes and individuals with active lifestyles.</p></p>
