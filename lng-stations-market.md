<p><p>LNG stations refer to facilities that provide liquefied natural gas for vehicles. There are two main market types for these stations: mobile stations and permanent stations. Mobile stations are temporary units that can be easily transported and set up at different locations to fulfill short-term demand. On the other hand, permanent stations are fixed installations that are built at specific locations to cater to long-term LNG supply needs. Both types serve the same purpose of delivering LNG for vehicles, but differ in their mobility and duration of operation.</p></p>
