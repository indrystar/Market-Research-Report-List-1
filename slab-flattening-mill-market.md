<p><p>Slab flattening mills are used to flatten wide, rough-cut slabs of wood. There are two main types of slab flattening mills: extended and basic. The extended slab flattening mill is designed for heavy-duty use and can handle large and thick slabs. It typically includes additional features like a longer bed and higher weight capacity. On the other hand, the basic slab flattening mill is more compact and suitable for smaller slabs. It is designed for lighter use and may lack some of the features found in the extended version.</p></p>
