<p><p>Cable water blocking tapes are specialized tapes used in the telecommunications and power cable industry to prevent water ingression into the cables. These tapes are classified into three types based on their conductivity properties. Conductive water blocking tapes are designed to provide a continuous electrical path to prevent water from reaching the cables. Semi-conductive tapes have moderate conductivity and restrict the propagation of water. Non-conductive tapes, on the other hand, do not conduct electricity and rely on their material properties to block water from reaching the cables.</p></p>
