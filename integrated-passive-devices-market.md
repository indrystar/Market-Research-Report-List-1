<p><strong>The Integrated Passive Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ESD</li><li>EMI</li><li>RF-IPD</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Passive Devices (IPDs) are compact components that combine multiple passive devices, such as inductors, capacitors, and resistors, into a single chip. IPDs can be categorized into different market types based on their applications. ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) IPDs are designed to protect electronic circuits from electrostatic discharges, ensuring their reliability and durability. EMI (Electromagnetic Interference) IPDs suppress unwanted electromagnetic disturbances, preventing interference and signal quality degradation. RF-IPDs are specifically designed for radio frequency applications, enabling wireless communication devices. Besides these, there are other IPD market types which cater to various specific needs such as filtering, sensing, and connectivity, among others.</p></p>
