<p><p>The malted milk powder market is categorized into three types based on the main ingredient used: wheat flour, barley, and other (whole milk) market. Wheat flour malted milk powder is made by combining malted barley extract with wheat flour. Barley malted milk powder is produced by mixing malted barley extract with barley flour. The "Other" category refers to malted milk powder made by combining malted barley extract with whole milk powder. These variations offer different flavors and nutritional profiles, providing consumers with a range of options to choose from.</p></p>
