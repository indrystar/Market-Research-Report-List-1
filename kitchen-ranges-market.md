<p><strong>The Kitchen Ranges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Freestanding Kitchen Range</li><li>Slide-in Kitchen Range</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen ranges are available in two main types: freestanding and slide-in. Freestanding kitchen ranges are versatile and can be placed anywhere in the kitchen as they have finished sides and back. They typically have a backsplash and controls on a panel at the back. On the other hand, slide-in kitchen ranges are designed to fit between cabinets and have a sleek built-in appearance. They do not have a backsplash and their controls are located at the front. Both types of ranges offer different options for homeowners based on their kitchen layout and design preferences.</p></p>
