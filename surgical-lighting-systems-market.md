<p><p>Surgical lighting systems market includes two main types: LED surgical lamps and halogen surgical lamps. LED surgical lamps utilize light-emitting diodes technology, providing high-quality illumination, longer lifespan, and energy efficiency. They offer improved visibility and color rendering, enhancing surgical precision. On the other hand, halogen surgical lamps use halogen bulbs to emit bright, focused light. Although less energy-efficient and have a shorter lifespan compared to LED lamps, halogen lamps are less expensive upfront. Both types strive to provide optimal lighting conditions during surgical procedures.</p></p>
