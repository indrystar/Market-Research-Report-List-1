<p><p>Stone extraction baskets are medical devices used during endoscopic procedures to retrieve stones or debris from the body, particularly from the urinary tract or the gallbladder. This growth can be attributed to factors such as the increasing prevalence of urolithiasis (urinary stone disease) and cholelithiasis (gallstone disease) worldwide. These conditions often require surgical intervention or endoscopic procedures, leading to a higher demand for stone extraction baskets.</p><p>Moreover, technological advancements in endoscopic procedures have also contributed to market growth. Modern stone extraction baskets are equipped with features such as higher torque control, improved navigation capabilities, and better grip, allowing for more precise and efficient stone retrieval.</p><p>In addition, the market is driven by the rising adoption of minimally invasive surgeries, as stone extraction baskets play a crucial role in such procedures. Minimally invasive techniques offer benefits such as smaller incisions, reduced post-operative pain, faster recovery time, and lower healthcare costs, which have led to increased preference for these procedures.</p><p>Furthermore, the stone extraction baskets market is witnessing various trends that are expected to influence its growth.
