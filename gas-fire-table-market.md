<p><p>Gas fire tables are a popular choice for outdoor heating and ambiance. There are two main types of gas fire tables based on the fuel they use: natural gas and propane. Natural gas fire tables are connected to a gas line, providing a continuous fuel source without the need for tanks or refills. Propane fire tables, on the other hand, use portable propane tanks for fuel, offering flexibility in placement but requiring occasional tank replacements. Both types offer convenience, ease of use, and a cozy ambiance for outdoor spaces.</p></p>
