<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Integrated Touchscreen Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Cell</li><li>In-Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Integrated Touchscreen Module market comprises two main types: On-Cell and In-Cell. On-Cell technology integrates the touch sensor layer above the display, allowing for thinner, lighter, and more cost-effective touchscreens. In comparison, In-Cell technology integrates the touch sensor layer within the display, resulting in even thinner and lighter screens with improved touch sensitivity. Both types eliminate the need for a separate touch panel, simplifying the manufacturing process and enhancing the overall user experience in various electronic devices, such as smartphones and tablets.</p></p>
