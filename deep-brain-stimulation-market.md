<p><ul><li>Implanted pulse generator</li><li>The lead</li><li>Extension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) market involves three main types of products. The implanted pulse generator (IPG) acts as the control unit and is implanted under the skin of the patient's chest or abdomen. The lead is a thin insulated wire that carries electrical pulses from the IPG to specific brain areas. It is placed through a small hole drilled into the skull. The extension market refers to the market for additional components connecting the IPG to the lead, providing flexibility and convenience for patients using DBS therapy.</p></p>
