<p><p>The Industrial Box PC market consists of two main types: standalone and embedded. Standalone Box PCs are complete computer systems that are designed to be placed on a desktop or rack, providing a self-contained solution for industrial applications. Embedded Box PCs, on the other hand, are compact computers that are integrated into larger systems or machinery, performing specific tasks within a specific environment.
