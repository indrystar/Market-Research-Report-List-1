<p><p>The Bird Detection System market consists of two types: Fixed Bird Detection System and Mobile Bird Detection System. Fixed Bird Detection System is a stationary setup typically installed in areas with high risks of bird strikes, such as airports. It uses radar or infrared technology to detect the presence of birds, providing real-time data to prevent collisions. On the other hand, Mobile Bird Detection System is a portable solution that can be easily moved to different locations. It is often used for temporary setups or in areas where a fixed system is not feasible. Both types aim to increase aviation safety by detecting and mitigating bird strike risks.</p></p>
