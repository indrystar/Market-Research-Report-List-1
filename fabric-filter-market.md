<p><ul><li>Pulse-Jet Cleaning</li><li>Shaking Cleaning</li><li>Reverse-Air Cleaning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fabric filter market includes various types of filtration systems such as pulse-jet cleaning, shaking cleaning, and reverse-air cleaning. Pulse-jet cleaning method involves using compressed air pulses to dislodge and remove dust particles from the filter bags. Shaking cleaning method involves mechanical shaking or vibrating of the filter bags to release accumulated dust. Reverse-air cleaning method uses a reverse airflow direction to clean the bags thoroughly. These different market types offer a range of options for industries to effectively and efficiently filter and separate particles from their processes.</p></p>
