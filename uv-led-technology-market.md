<p><p>UV LED technology market types can be categorized into UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA LEDs emit ultraviolet light with a longer wavelength, which is commonly used in UV curing, counterfeit detection, and phototherapy. UVB LEDs have a shorter wavelength and are primarily used in phototherapy for treating skin conditions, such as psoriasis and eczema. Lastly, UVC LEDs emit the shortest wavelength and are utilized for disinfection purposes, including water and air sterilization, surface and object sanitization, and in healthcare settings to kill harmful microorganisms.</p></p>
