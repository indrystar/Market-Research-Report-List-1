Car roof bars provide a convenient and secure way to transport such equipment.</p><p>Secondly, the growing trend of adventure tourism and road trips has also contributed to the market growth. Consumers are increasingly seeking experiences and are opting for self-drive tours or road trips, thereby requiring additional storage on their vehicles.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing production and sale of SUVs and crossover vehicles has boosted the demand for car roof bars. These vehicles often have limited trunk space, and car roof bars offer a solution for carrying extra luggage or equipment.</p><p>In terms of trends, manufacturers are focusing on producing lightweight and aerodynamic car roof bars to minimize fuel consumption and wind noise. Additionally, there is a rising preference for roof bars with adjustable and foldable features for easy installation and space-saving storage.</p><p>In conclusion, the car roof bar market is expanding steadily and is projected to grow at a CAGR of 9.9% during the forecast period.
