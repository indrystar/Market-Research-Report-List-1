<p><p>The green renewable energy market encompasses various types, namely Solar Photovoltaic (PV), Wind Energy, Hydroelectric Power, Bio-fuels, and Geothermal Energy. Solar PV involves harnessing sunlight through photovoltaic cells to generate electricity. Wind energy harnesses wind power using turbines to produce electricity. Hydroelectric power utilizes the force of flowing or falling water to generate electricity. Bio-fuels are derived from organic materials like plants or animal waste, providing an alternative to fossil fuels. Geothermal energy uses heat from the Earth's core to generate power. These renewable energy sources aim to reduce reliance on non-renewable resources while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
