<p><p>Neurology devices encompass various types of medical devices used to diagnose, monitor, and treat neurological disorders. Neuro-stimulation devices include deep brain stimulation and spinal cord stimulation devices that provide electrical stimulation to alleviate symptoms. Interventional devices are used for minimally invasive procedures like embolization or angioplasty in the brain. Cerebral spinal fluid management devices treat conditions like hydrocephalus, while neurosurgery devices are utilized during surgical procedures on the brain or spinal cord. The "Others" category includes devices such as neurodiagnostic devices and neurotherapeutic devices for specific treatments like neurorehabilitation or neurofeedback.</p></p>
