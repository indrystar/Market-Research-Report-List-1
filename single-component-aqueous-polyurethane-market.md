<p><p>Single-component aqueous polyurethanes are used in various applications such as coatings, adhesives, sealants, and elastomers. The two main types of single-component aqueous polyurethanes are aqueous polyurethane dispersion and aqueous polyurethane emulsion. Aqueous polyurethane dispersions are colloidal suspensions of polyurethane particles dispersed in water, while aqueous polyurethane emulsions are stable mixtures of polyurethane and water achieved through emulsification. Both types offer advantages such as low VOC emissions, excellent durability, and flexibility, making them popular choices in environmentally-friendly coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
