<p><p>A servo motor is a precise and efficient motor that uses feedback to control the position, speed, and acceleration of a mechanical system. <p><ul><li>D.C. Servo-Motors</li><li>A.C. Servo-Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Servo motors are classified into two main types: D.C. servo motors and A.C. servo motors. D.C. servo motors are powered by direct current and commonly used in applications where precise control of position and speed is required, such as robotics and industrial automation. On the other hand, A.C. servo motors are powered by alternating current and offer high torque-to-inertia ratio, making them suitable for dynamic applications like CNC machines and servo drives. Both types are integral components in various industries, providing accurate and efficient motion control solutions.</p></p>
