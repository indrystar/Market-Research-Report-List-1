<p><p>A central venous catheter (CVC) is a medical device used to administer medication or fluids, obtain blood samples, or measure central venous pressure. It is inserted into a large vein in the neck, chest, or groin area and is typically used for patients who require long-term intravenous access or frequent blood draws. A central venous catheter kit includes all the necessary components for the insertion and maintenance of a CVC, such as catheters, guidewires, dilators, and syringes.</p><p>The global central venous catheter kits market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, the rising number of surgical procedures, and the growing demand for advanced healthcare facilities. Additionally, the adoption of technologically advanced products and the rising geriatric population are fueling market growth.</p><p>The market is also influenced by the increasing focus on patient safety and reducing infections associated with catheter use. Manufacturers are developing CVCs with enhanced features such as antimicrobial coatings and anti-thrombogenic properties to minimize the risk of infection and thrombosis.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising demand for minimally invasive procedures and the introduction of innovative CVC kits are driving market growth.
