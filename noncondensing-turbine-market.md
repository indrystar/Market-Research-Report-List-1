<p><p>A noncondensing turbine is a type of turbine that operates without utilizing a condenser to convert steam back into water. Noncondensing turbines are often smaller in size and are more suitable for decentralized power generation systems.</p><p>The Noncondensing Turbine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
