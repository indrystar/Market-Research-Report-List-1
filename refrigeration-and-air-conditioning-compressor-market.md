<p><p>The refrigeration and air conditioning compressor market comprises different types of compressors, including rotary-screw and reciprocating compressors. Rotary-screw compressors utilize a pair of interlocking helical rotors to compress refrigerant gas. These compressors are known for their efficiency and smooth operation. On the other hand, reciprocating compressors work by using a piston-cylinder mechanism to compress refrigerant through linear motion. They are commonly used in smaller applications and known for their ability to handle high pressure ratios. These two types of compressors provide essential functions in various refrigeration and air conditioning systems.</p></p>
