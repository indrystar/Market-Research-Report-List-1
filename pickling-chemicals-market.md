<p><p>Pickling chemicals market can be classified into two types: aqueous solution and concentrated solution. Aqueous solutions are made by dissolving pickling chemicals in water, resulting in a diluted concentration of the chemicals. This type is commonly used for household purposes or in industries where a less potent and corrosive solution is required. On the other hand, concentrated solutions contain a higher concentration of pickling chemicals, making them more powerful and suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications where strong adhesion removal is needed.</p></p>
