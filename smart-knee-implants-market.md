<p><p>Smart knee implants, also known as intelligent knee implants or computer-assisted knee implants, are innovative medical devices that integrate sensors, microprocessors, and actuators to provide real-time data and adjust the implant's behavior according to the patient's needs. These implants offer enhanced functionality and personalized treatment options for individuals suffering from knee joint degeneration or injury.</p><p>The smart knee implants market is witnessing substantial growth due to several factors. The rising prevalence of knee-related disorders, such as osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis, coupled with the increasing geriatric population globally, is driving the demand for smart knee implants. These implants provide improved accuracy and stability during surgery, leading to better patient outcomes and shorter recovery periods.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements in smart knee implants, such as the use of artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms, machine learning, and real-time monitoring capabilities, are fueling market growth. These advancements enable continuous monitoring of knee joint functionality, detection of implant-related complications, and personalized pain management.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness among both patients and healthcare providers about the benefits of smart knee implants is further propelling market expansion.
