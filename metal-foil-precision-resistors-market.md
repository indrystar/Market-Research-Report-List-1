<p><p>The Metal Foil Precision Resistors market can be categorized into two main types: SMD Type and Through Hole Type. SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type resistors are small-sized and designed to be mounted on the surface of a printed circuit board (PCB) using automated assembly processes. On the other hand, Through Hole Type resistors have leads that are inserted through holes on the PCB and soldered on the bottom side. Both types of resistors are used for precise resistance values in electronic circuits but differ in their mounting and assembly methods.</p></p>
