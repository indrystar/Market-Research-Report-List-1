<p><p>The in-duct air purifiers market is segmented into three types: UV type, HEPA, and others. UV type air purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to neutralize airborne pathogens, eliminating bacteria, viruses, and molds. HEPA air purifiers use high-efficiency particulate air filters to trap and remove dust, pollen, pet dander, and other allergens from the air. The "others" category includes air purifiers with different technologies such as activated carbon filters, ionizers, or electrostatic precipitators. These different types of air purifiers cater to a variety of indoor air quality needs in homes, offices, and other spaces.</p></p>
