<p><p>The indoor electromagnetic lock market consists of two main types: mounting electromagnetic locks and embedded electromagnetic locks. </p><p>Mounting electromagnetic locks are installed on the surface of a door frame, with the lock electromagnet mounted on the frame and the armature plate mounted on the door itself. This type of lock is commonly used in commercial buildings and offers high security.</p><p>Embedded electromagnetic locks, on the other hand, are installed within the door frame. The lock electromagnet is concealed within the frame, and the armature plate is embedded within the door. This type of lock provides a seamless and hidden security solution, often used in high-end residential or corporate environments.</p></p>
