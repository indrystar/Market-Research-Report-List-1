<p><p>The medical digital thermometer market consists of two main types: electronic and infrared thermometers. Electronic thermometers use electronic sensors to measure body temperature, and the readings are displayed on a digital screen. They are typically placed under the tongue, in the armpit, or in the rectum for accurate readings. On the other hand, infrared thermometers measure temperature through the forehead or ear canal using infrared technology. They provide quick and non-invasive temperature readings, making them ideal for use in hospitals, clinics, and homes.</p></p>
