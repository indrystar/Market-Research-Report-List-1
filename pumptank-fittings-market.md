<p><p>The Pump/Tank Fittings market consists of various types of fittings used in industrial processes. IBC couplings are used to connect Intermediate Bulk Containers to pumps or tanks. Sweeps are curved fittings that promote the smooth flow of fluids. Flow meters measure the rate of flow in a pipe or tubing. Dry disconnects are quick-connect fittings that prevent spills when disconnecting hoses. The "Others" category includes various fittings like valves, adapters, and strainers. These fittings play a crucial role in ensuring the efficiency, safety, and controlled flow of fluids in pumping and tank systems.</p></p>
