<p><p>The adult powdered milk market can be categorized into two types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder is made by evaporating liquid milk until it becomes a powder, preserving the natural fats found in milk. It is rich in nutrients such as vitamins, minerals, and proteins. On the other hand, skim milk powder is made by removing the fat content from liquid milk, resulting in a powder that is lower in fat but still contains essential nutrients. Both types cater to the needs of adults seeking convenient and nutritious milk options.</p></p>
