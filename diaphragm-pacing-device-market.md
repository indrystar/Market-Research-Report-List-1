<p><p>A Diaphragm Pacing Device (DPD) is a medical device used to assist individuals with breathing difficulties caused by the paralysis or weakness of the diaphragm muscle. It stimulates the diaphragm muscle to contract and relax through electrical impulses, thereby improving the patient's ability to breathe.
