This structure helps to reduce recombination losses and enhance the efficiency of solar cells by improving light absorption.</p><p>The Heterojunction With Intrinsic Thin-Layer market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period. <p><p>Heterojunction with Intrinsic Thin-layer (HIT) technology is utilized in the market for solar cells. The HIT cells consist of two different semiconductor layers - P-type and N-type junctions. The P-type region has a higher concentration of positive charge carriers (holes), while the N-type region has more negative charge carriers (electrons). The junction between these two layers creates an electric field, allowing efficient charge separation and reduction of recombination losses. These two market types, P-type and N-type, differ in the type of doping used, which helps in optimizing the performance and efficiency of HIT solar cells.</p></p>
