<p><strong>The Horizontal End Suction Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Mounted End Suction Pump</li><li>Close Coupled End Suction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horizontal end suction pump market consists of two main types: frame mounted end suction pump and close coupled end suction pump. </p><p>Frame mounted end suction pumps are designed with the pump and motor mounted on a common baseplate, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications that require high flow rates and high heads. They are commonly used in industries such as water treatment, irrigation, and HVAC.</p><p>Close coupled end suction pumps have the pump directly coupled to the motor, eliminating the need for a separate coupling or baseplate. This type of pump is more compact and space-efficient, making it suitable for smaller applications such as building services, domestic water supply, and light industrial use.</p></p>
