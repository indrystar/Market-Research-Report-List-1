<p><p>There are two main types of hour meters available in the market - analogue and digital. Analogue hour meters have a traditional dial display similar to a clock, where the hours are physically represented with hands. On the other hand, digital hour meters use electronic digit displays to showcase the hours. While analogue meter readings require manual interpretation, digital meters provide direct and accurate readings. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications where tracking or recording running time is essential.</p></p>
