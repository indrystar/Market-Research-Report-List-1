Scintillation detectors use crystals to convert radiation energy into light, providing accurate measurements for gamma and beta radiation. Neutron detectors are designed to detect and measure neutron radiation. Geiger counters use gas-filled chambers to detect and measure radiation, making them effective for a wide range of radiation types. Additionally, there are other types of detectors available in the market offering unique features and capabilities to cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
