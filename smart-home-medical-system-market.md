<p><p>The Smart Home Medical System market consists of two main types: WiFi and Bluetooth. WiFi-enabled medical systems utilize wireless internet connectivity, allowing users to remotely monitor their health condition and receive real-time health updates from any location. Bluetooth-enabled medical systems, on the other hand, use short-range wireless technology to connect medical devices to a central hub or smartphone, enabling seamless data sharing and tracking. Both types offer convenient and efficient ways to manage personal health at home, providing users with more control and accessibility to their medical data.</p></p>
