<p><p>Tourniquet systems refer to medical devices used to temporarily restrict blood flow to a specific area of the body during surgeries or in emergency situations. The market types within the tourniquet systems include tourniquet instruments and tourniquet cuffs. Tourniquet instruments are the tools used to apply and control the pressure exerted by the tourniquet cuff on the body part. Tourniquet cuffs, on the other hand, are the inflatable or non-inflatable bands that are placed around the limb to restrict blood flow. Both instrument and cuff components are essential for an effective tourniquet system.</p></p>
