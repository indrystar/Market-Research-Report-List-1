<p><strong>The Portable Mixers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive</li><li>Gear Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Mixers market includes two main types: Direct Drive and Gear Drive mixers. Direct Drive mixers have a motor directly connected to the mixing impeller, providing a simpler and more compact design, and often requiring less maintenance. On the other hand, Gear Drive mixers use a system of gears to transmit power from the motor to the mixing impeller, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications and offering greater torque control. Both types are widely used in various industries for mixing and blending purposes.</p></p>
