<p><p>Glass insulators are used in electrical power transmission systems to support and insulate overhead wires. There are two main types of glass insulators in the market: suspension glass insulators and pin glass insulators. Suspension glass insulators are designed to hang vertically from a suspension hardware and are used in high-voltage transmission lines. Pin glass insulators are mounted on a pin and provide support for low-voltage distribution lines. Both types of glass insulators are crucial in ensuring the safe and efficient flow of electricity in power transmission networks.</p></p>
