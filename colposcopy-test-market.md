These systems offer enhanced visualization, better image quality, and the ability to capture and store images for further analysis. This technology is particularly beneficial in remote areas where access to specialized healthcare is limited. Additionally, automated diagnostic algorithms are being developed to assist colposcopists in the interpretation of colposcopy images, further improving accuracy and reducing subjectivity in diagnosis.</p><p>Another trend is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) in colposcopy.
