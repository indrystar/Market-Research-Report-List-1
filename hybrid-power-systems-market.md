<p><p>Hybrid Power Systems combine renewable energy sources such as solar and wind with conventional diesel generators to provide electricity. In a Solar-Diesel system, solar panels generate electricity during the day, and diesel generators are used as backup during non-sunny periods. Similarly, Wind-Diesel systems utilize wind turbines to generate power, supplemented by diesel generators when wind speeds are low. Wind-Solar-Diesel systems incorporate all three sources, leveraging solar panels, wind turbines, and diesel generators to ensure continuous electricity supply even during periods of low solar radiation and wind.</p></p>
