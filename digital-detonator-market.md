<p><p>The Digital Detonator market can be classified into two types: Instantaneous Electric Detonator and Delay Electric Detonator. Instantaneous Electric Detonators are designed to provide immediate detonation upon activation, ensuring precise and accurate timing. On the other hand, Delay Electric Detonators are equipped with time delays, allowing users to control the time of detonation. Both types offer advanced digital capabilities, enabling remote initiation and increased safety measures. These digital detonators play a crucial role in industries such as mining, construction, and military, providing efficient and controlled explosive operations.</p></p>
