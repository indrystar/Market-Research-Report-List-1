<p><strong>The Cultured Whey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Isolate</li><li>Whey Hydrolysate</li><li>Whey Concentrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cultured whey market comprises three primary market types: whey isolate, whey hydrolysate, and whey concentrate. Whey isolate is a pure form of whey protein that goes through a filtration process, removing most of the lactose and fat. Whey hydrolysate undergoes an additional step of enzymatic hydrolysis, breaking down whey protein into smaller peptides for easier digestion. Whey concentrate contains a moderate level of protein and retains more lactose and fat compared to the other two types. These market types cater to different consumer preferences and dietary needs within the whey protein industry.</p></p>
