<p><ul><li>HIFU</li><li>MRgFUS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Tissue Ablation System market includes two types: High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) and Magnetic Resonance guided Focused Ultrasound Surgery (MRgFUS). HIFU uses high intensity ultrasound waves to generate heat and destroy targeted tissues without the need for incisions. MRgFUS combines ultrasound imaging with MRI guidance to provide real-time visualization and precise targeting of tissues, enabling non-invasive ablation. Both technologies offer minimally invasive alternatives for tissue ablation procedures, providing patients with less pain, shorter recovery times, and improved outcomes.</p></p>
