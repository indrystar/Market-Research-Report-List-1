<p><p>The HVAC control valve market consists of various types of valves used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. Static balancing valves are used to regulate the flow of water in the system and maintain a balanced distribution. Dynamic balancing valves provide automatic regulation of flow based on system requirements. Pressure Independent Control Valves (PICV) combine the functions of both static and dynamic valves, ensuring constant flow regardless of pressure changes. These valves play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and performance of HVAC systems in various applications.</p></p>
