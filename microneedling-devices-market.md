<p><p>Microneedling devices are a popular skincare treatment that use tiny needles to create small puncture wounds in the skin, stimulating collagen production and promoting skin rejuvenation. There are three main types of microneedling devices in the market: derma-stamp, dermapen, and dermarollers. A derma-stamp features a stamp-like design with microneedles that penetrate the skin evenly. The dermapen is a handheld device with a cluster of microneedles that move up and down, offering precise and controlled treatment. Dermarollers consist of a roller with microneedles that are rolled across the skin, making it ideal for larger treatment areas.</p></p>
