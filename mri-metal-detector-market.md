<p><p>An MRI metal detector is a specialized device used in medical imaging to ensure patient safety during an MRI scan. MRI scanners use powerful magnets to generate detailed images of the body's internal structures. However, these magnets can pose a significant risk as they can attract nearby metal objects, causing serious injury or damage. MRI metal detectors are designed to detect any metal objects on a person before they enter the MRI room, ensuring that no harmful incidents occur.</p><p>The future outlook of the MRI metal detector market looks promising, with a forecasted compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.3% during the projected period.
