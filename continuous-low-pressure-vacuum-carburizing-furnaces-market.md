<p><p>Continuous low pressure vacuum carburizing furnaces can be categorized into three types: single chamber, double chamber, and multi chamber.</p><p>- Single chamber furnaces have only one working chamber where the carburizing process takes place.</p><p>- Double chamber furnaces consist of two chambers: one for carburizing and one for cooling and quenching.</p><p>- Multi chamber furnaces have multiple chambers, which allow for simultaneous carburizing and other processes, such as hardening or tempering.</p><p>These different types of furnaces enable manufacturers to choose the most suitable configuration based on their specific production requirements and desired outcomes.</p></p>
