<p><p>The vacuum breaker market can be divided into two main types: low pressure type and high pressure type. The low pressure type vacuum breakers are designed to alleviate negative pressure in a system operating under low pressure conditions, preventing backflow or contamination. On the other hand, high pressure vacuum breakers are utilized in systems exposed to high pressure levels and are instrumental in preventing the formation of vacuum conditions, which could lead to pipe collapse or other damage. These two types cater to specific pressure requirements in different applications.</p></p>
