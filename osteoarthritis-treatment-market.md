<p><p>Osteoarthritis is a degenerative joint disease that commonly affects the elderly population. It primarily occurs due to the wear and tear of joint cartilage, resulting in pain, stiffness, and reduced movement. The goal of osteoarthritis treatment is to alleviate symptoms, maintain joint function, and improve the quality of life for patients.</p><p>Treatment options for osteoarthritis include both non-pharmacological and pharmacological interventions. Non-pharmacological treatments focus on lifestyle modifications, weight management, exercise, physiotherapy, and assistive devices. Pharmacological treatments include the use of analgesics, nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), corticosteroids, and hyaluronic acid injections. In severe cases, joint replacement surgery may be considered.</p><p>The global osteoarthritis treatment market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
