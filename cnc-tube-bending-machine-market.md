<p><p>The CNC tube bending machine market consists of three main types: fully electric CNC pipe benders, hybrid CNC pipe benders, and hydraulic NC pipe benders. Fully electric CNC pipe benders use electric motors to control all movements, providing high precision and energy efficiency. Hybrid CNC pipe benders combine electric and hydraulic systems for improved productivity and accuracy. Hydraulic NC pipe benders rely solely on hydraulic systems, offering high force capabilities but lower precision. These machines find applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>
