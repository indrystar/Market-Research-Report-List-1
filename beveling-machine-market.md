<p><p>A beveling machine is a tool used to create beveled edges on metal plates and pipes, which are often required for welding purposes. It accomplishes this by removing material from the edges to create a slope or angle. This enables a smoother and stronger weld joint by providing more surface area for the weld to adhere to.</p><p>The beveling machine market is expected to experience steady growth during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the expanding construction and manufacturing sectors globally are driving the demand for beveling machines. These machines are essential for fabricating various metal structures and components used in construction and manufacturing processes. Additionally, the rising adoption of automation in industrial operations is further driving the market. Automation helps in improving efficiency, accuracy, and productivity, thereby increasing the demand for beveling machines.</p><p>Another significant trend observed in the beveling machine market is the integration of advanced technologies. Manufacturers are incorporating features such as computer numerical control (CNC), robotics, and artificial intelligence (AI) in beveling machines to enhance their performance and precision. These technological advancements allow for greater automation, accuracy, and customization, thereby propelling market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing focus on sustainable and eco-friendly manufacturing processes is expected to drive the adoption of electric and hybrid beveling machines. These machines offer reduced emissions, noise levels, and energy consumption compared to traditional beveling machines powered by internal combustion engines.</p><p>Overall, with the increasing demand for advanced technologies, the beveling machine market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 3.20% during the forecast period.</p></p>

<p><p>The beveling machine market is divided into two main types: stationary and portable machines.
