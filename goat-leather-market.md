<p><strong>The Goat Leather Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dried</li><li>Raw</li><li>Tanned Or Crust</li><li>Wet-Blue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The goat leather market can be categorized into four types based on the stage of processing: dried, raw, tanned or crust, and wet-blue. Dried goat leather refers to goat hides that have been dried naturally, preserving the leather's quality. Raw goat leather is untreated and has not undergone any tanning process. Tanned or crust goat leather has undergone tanning, which is the process of treating the hides with chemicals to improve durability and appearance. Wet-blue goat leather refers to hides that have been partially tanned, leaving them with a blueish hue due to the presence of chromium salts.</p></p>
