<p><p>The neurovascular intervention coil market offers different types of coils used in the treatment of brain conditions. Coil assist stents are devices that combine stents and coils to treat aneurysms by promoting blood clotting. Flow diverter stents are employed to redirect blood flow and prevent aneurysms from rupturing. Micro wire/catheters are thin wires or tubes used to navigate through blood vessels and deliver coils or stents to the affected area. These devices aid in the minimally invasive treatment of neurovascular disorders, offering improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
