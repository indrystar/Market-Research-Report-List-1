<p><p>The Urodynamic Devices Market consists of various types of devices used in the diagnosis and treatment of urological disorders. Ambulatory Urodynamic Devices are portable and allow for testing outside of the clinic. Cystometers measure bladder pressure and volume during filling and emptying. Video Urodynamic Devices combine cystometry with fluoroscopy for real-time imaging. Electromyography measures muscle activity during bladder function tests. Urodynamic Catheters are used to measure pressure and flow rates. Urodynamic Pumps provide controlled filling and emptying of the bladder. Transducer Sets convert pressure signals into electrical signals for measurement.</p></p>
