<p><p>A quasi continuous wave (CW) fiber laser is a type of laser that operates in a pulsed manner but with a high repetition rate, typically in the kilohertz range. These lasers are widely used in various applications such as material processing, medical surgery, scientific research, and telecommunications.</p><p>The future outlook for the quasi CW fiber laser market is highly promising.
