<p><p>Hopper scales are used to weigh bulk materials in various industries. There are two types of hopper scales: double bucket and single bucket. In a double bucket market, two separate buckets are used to collect and weigh the material. This is suitable for large quantities as it allows continuous loading and weighing. On the other hand, in a single bucket market, only one bucket is used for both loading and weighing. This is more suitable for smaller quantities and is a more cost-effective solution.</p></p>
