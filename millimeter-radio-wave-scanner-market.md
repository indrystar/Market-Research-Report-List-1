<p><strong>The Millimeter Radio-Wave Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Scanner</li><li>Active Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The millimeter radio-wave scanner market comprises two main types: passive and active scanners. A passive scanner detects radio waves emitting from objects, analyzing the energy they reflect or emit naturally. This type is commonly used for security purposes, identifying concealed items without emitting any signal. Active scanners, on the other hand, emit their own radio waves and analyze the reflections to form an image. This type is utilized in various applications, including security checkpoints and medical imaging systems, where a higher level of accuracy is required.</p></p>
