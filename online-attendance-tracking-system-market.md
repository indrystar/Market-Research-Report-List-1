<p><p>The Online Attendance Tracking System market can be classified into two main types: Cloud-based and On-premises. Cloud-based attendance tracking systems operate on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage data online. They offer scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, On-premises attendance tracking systems are locally installed on a company's servers, providing full control over data management and security. They are typically used by organizations that prefer to keep their data within their own infrastructure but require higher upfront costs and maintenance.</p></p>
