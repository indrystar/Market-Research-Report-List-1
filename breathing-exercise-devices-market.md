<p><ul><li>Three Flow Breathing Exercise Devices</li><li>Two Flow Breathing Exercise Devices</li><li>Single Flow Breathing Exercise Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breathing exercise devices are segmented into three types: Three Flow, Two Flow, and Single Flow devices. Three Flow devices allow users to adjust the inhalation, exhalation, and pause times, offering a customizable experience. Two Flow devices provide adjustable inhalation and exhalation settings, but lack the pause feature. Single Flow devices offer a fixed inhalation and exhalation rate without any adjustment options. These devices are designed to help individuals practice controlled breathing techniques and improve respiratory health.</p></p>
