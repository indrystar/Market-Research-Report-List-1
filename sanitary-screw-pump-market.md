<p><p>The Sanitary Screw Pump market consists of two main types: Vertical Screw Pump and Horizontal Screw Pump. Vertical Screw Pumps are designed to operate with the motor located at the top, making them ideal for situations where space is limited. They are commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and chemical. On the other hand, Horizontal Screw Pumps have a horizontal orientation and are more suitable for applications involving high-viscosity fluids. These pumps find extensive use in industries like oil and gas, wastewater treatment, and marine. Both types provide efficient and reliable pumping solutions for different industry needs.</p></p>
