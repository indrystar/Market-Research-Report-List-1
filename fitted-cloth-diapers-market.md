<p><p>Fitted cloth diapers are available in two main types: single-layer and multi-layer. Single-layer diapers consist of a single layer of absorbent fabric, offering simplicity and quick drying time. On the other hand, multi-layer diapers are made up of multiple layers of absorbent material, providing enhanced absorbency and leakage protection. These diapers are designed to fit snugly on a baby's body, allowing for better containment of moisture and providing a more comfortable fit. Both types offer parents a sustainable and cost-effective alternative to disposable diapers.</p></p>
