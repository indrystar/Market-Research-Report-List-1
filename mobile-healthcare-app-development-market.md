<p><p>Mobile healthcare app development market can be classified into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise apps are installed and run directly on the user's device, without relying on an internet connection. Users have more control and privacy over their data, but updates and maintenance may require manual intervention. On the other hand, cloud-based apps store data on remote servers accessible through an internet connection. They offer scalability, easier updates, and synchronization across multiple devices, but they may raise security and privacy concerns for some users.</p></p>
