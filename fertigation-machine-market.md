<p><p>Fertigation refers to the practice of applying fertilizers and other nutrients to crops through an irrigation system. Factors driving this growth include the increasing adoption of modern irrigation techniques, the rising need to optimize fertilization processes, and the growing demand for high crop yields. These features enable farmers to monitor and regulate the fertilization process from a remote location, leading to enhanced operational efficiency.</p><p>In terms of trends, precision agriculture is gaining traction in the fertigation machine market. The integration of sensing technologies, data analytics, and GPS systems allows farmers to precisely monitor the nutrient requirements of crops and apply fertilizers accordingly.</p><p>Furthermore, the advent of smart fertigation systems that can optimize nutrient dosages based on real-time data is also shaping the market. These systems utilize sensors to measure soil moisture, temperature, and nutrient levels to provide accurate recommendations for fertilization.</p><p>Overall, the fertigation machine market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period.
