<p><p>TVR Rising Film Evaporators market types include Single Effect, Dual Effect, Triple Effect, and Others. A Single Effect evaporator uses steam to heat the liquid being evaporated. Dual Effect evaporators use the vapor produced from the first effect to heat the liquid in the second effect, increasing efficiency. Triple Effect evaporators further increase efficiency by using the vapor from the second effect to heat the liquid in the third effect. The Others category includes unconventional or customized systems that do not fit into the traditional categories. These different types cater to varying needs and requirements in the evaporation process.</p></p>
