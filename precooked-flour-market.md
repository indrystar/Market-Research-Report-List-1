<p><p>The Precooked Flour Market encompasses various types, including Rice, Maize, Wheat, Legumes, and Others. Rice-based precooked flour is made from rice grains and is widely used in Asian cuisines. Maize-based precooked flour is derived from corn and finds applications in tortillas, snacks, and bakery products. Wheat-based precooked flour is commonly used in the bread, pasta, and bakery industries. Legumes-based precooked flour, obtained from beans or lentils, is a rich source of protein and used in gluten-free products. Lastly, "Others" include precooked flours made from alternative grains or ingredients like quinoa, buckwheat, or potato starch.</p></p>
