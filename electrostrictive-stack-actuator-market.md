Pre-stressed electrostrictive stack actuators have an internal stress applied during manufacturing, enabling them to achieve high displacement and force output. On the other hand, the market also offers no pre-stress electrostrictive stack actuators, which lack the initial internal stress but still provide displacement and force outputs suitable for certain applications. Both types cater to different requirements and provide options for engineers in selecting the appropriate actuator for their specific needs.</p></p>
