These devices have become ubiquitous in healthcare settings globally.</p><p>The syringes and cannulas market has witnessed substantial growth in recent years and is expected to continue growing at a CAGR of 8.20% during the forecast period. Furthermore, the growing geriatric population and rising healthcare expenditure worldwide contribute to market growth.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology, such as the development of retractable safety syringes to prevent needlestick injuries and the innovative design of nanocapillary tubes for precision drug delivery, have also propelled the market forward. Moreover, the increasing adoption of syringes and cannulas in home healthcare settings and the rise in self-administration of medications further boost market growth.</p><p>In terms of market trends, the shift towards the usage of prefilled syringes is gaining traction. Furthermore, the integration of technologies like Bluetooth and Near Field Communication (NFC) in syringes to enable connectivity and track medication adherence has become more prevalent.</p><p>In conclusion, the syringes and cannulas market is poised for significant growth, driven by factors such as the rising prevalence of chronic diseases, technological advancements, and the increasing demand for home healthcare.
