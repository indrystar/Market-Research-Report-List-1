<p><p>The RF Digital Multimeter market can be categorized into three main types: Benchtop, Portable, and Modular. </p><p>Benchtop digital multimeters are larger, stationary devices primarily used in laboratory settings for high-precision and complex measurements. They offer a wide range of features and have a higher accuracy compared to other types.</p><p>Portable digital multimeters are compact and lightweight, designed for on-the-go applications. They are suitable for fieldwork and electrical troubleshooting tasks due to their portability and ease of use.</p><p>Modular digital multimeters consist of different modules that can be connected and customized, offering flexibility and scalability for specialized applications. They are commonly utilized in automated test systems and production environments.</p></p>
