<p><p>Intelligent Virtual Assistants (IVAs) are categorized into two main types: Rule Based and Conversational AI Based. Rule Based IVAs follow predetermined sets of rules to gather and provide information, mostly through pre-defined responses. On the other hand, Conversational AI Based IVAs employ advanced technologies like machine learning and natural language processing to understand user queries in a more dynamic and human-like way, allowing for contextually relevant responses. These two types differ in their underlying knowledge bases and the level of personalization and interactivity they can offer to users.</p></p>
