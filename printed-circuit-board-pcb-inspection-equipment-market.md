<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Inspection Equipment Market includes different types of equipment used for inspecting PCBs. Two widely used types are Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) and Automatic X-Ray Inspection (AXI). AOI systems use high-resolution cameras to visually inspect the PCBs for defects such as misalignments or missing components. AXI systems, on the other hand, utilize X-ray technology to inspect hidden solder joints or analyze internal structures. Both types of equipment provide accurate and reliable inspection results, enhancing the quality control process in PCB manufacturing.</p></p>
