<p><p>Spiral dental implant market types include cone, conical, and cylindrical implants. Cone implants have a tapered shape resembling a cone, providing stability and increased surface area for bone integration. Conical implants have a wider base and a narrower apex, offering excellent primary stability and improved initial anchorage. Cylindrical implants have a uniform diameter throughout their length and are most commonly used due to their simplicity and predictable success rates. These different types cater to the diverse needs of patients and dentists, offering various options for reliable dental implant solutions.</p></p>
