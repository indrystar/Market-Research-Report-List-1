<p><p>The Portable Colorimeter market offers various types of colorimeters for different color spaces. For CIE LAB, colorimeters are designed specifically for measuring color coordinates in the LAB color space, which represents color in terms of lightness, green-red, and blue-yellow components. For XYZ, colorimeters are used to measure color values in the XYZ color space, which describes color as a combination of three primary colors. For LCh, colorimeters are utilized to measure color attributes in the LCh color space, which encompasses lightness, chroma, and hue. Similarly, for RGB and LUV, specific colorimeters are available to measure color in their respective color spaces.</p></p>
