<p><p>Glossmeter market includes three types: Single Angle, Two Angles, and Multi Angles. Single Angle glossmeters measure glossiness by emitting light at a specific angle and measuring the reflected light intensity, suitable for simple gloss assessment. Two Angles glossmeters measure glossiness at two different angles to provide improved accuracy, particularly for metallic surfaces. Multi Angles glossmeters employ multiple angles for measurement, allowing the assessment of glossiness variations under different lighting conditions, making them useful for complex surfaces and ensuring accurate gloss assessment in various industries.</p></p>
