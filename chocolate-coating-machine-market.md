<p><p>The market for chocolate coating machines can be categorized into two main types: Automatic Chocolate Coating Machines and Semi-Automatic Chocolate Coating Machines. Automatic machines are fully automated and perform all coating processes without the need for human intervention. They are more efficient and can handle large quantities of chocolates.
