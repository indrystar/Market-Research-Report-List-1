<p><ul><li>Dynamic Flow Chemisorption Analyzer</li><li>Static Chemisorption Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemisorption analyzer market comprises two types: Dynamic Flow Chemisorption Analyzer and Static Chemisorption Analyzer. Dynamic Flow Chemisorption Analyzer is a device that measures the adsorption and desorption of gases on a solid surface under dynamic conditions. It provides information on surface area, pore size distribution, and active site concentration. On the other hand, Static Chemisorption Analyzer measures the chemisorption of gases on a solid surface under static conditions. It helps in determining the active site concentration and the strength of chemical bonds on the surface. Both types play a crucial role in analyzing the characteristics of solid surfaces.</p></p>
