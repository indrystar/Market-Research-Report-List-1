<p><strong>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Edge Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) market comprises two main types: Edge Type and Direct Type. Edge Type BLUs are designed with LEDs placed around the edges of the LCD panel, utilizing light diffusers to evenly distribute the light across the screen. These BLUs are slim, cost-effective, and widely used in thinner displays such as televisions and computer monitors. On the other hand, Direct Type BLUs use LEDs positioned directly behind the LCD panel, offering more precise lighting control and superior image quality for high-end displays like smartphones and tablets.</p></p>
