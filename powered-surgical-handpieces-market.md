<p><ul><li>Electric-Powered</li><li>Battery-Powered</li><li>Pneumatic-Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powered surgical handpieces market consists of three main types: electric-powered, battery-powered, and pneumatic-powered handpieces. Electric-powered handpieces are connected to a power source and provide constant high-speed rotation, offering precision and control during surgical procedures. Battery-powered handpieces offer flexibility and portability as they run on rechargeable batteries. They are commonly used in remote or mobile settings. Pneumatic-powered handpieces utilize compressed air to generate rotational movement, providing a lightweight and low-vibration option for surgical procedures. These different types cater to specific needs and preferences in the medical field.</p></p>
