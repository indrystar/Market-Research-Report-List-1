<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Edge Controller market can be divided into two types: DIN-rail and Panel Mount. DIN-rail controllers are designed to be mounted on a standardized metal rail commonly used in industrial environments. They are compact, sturdy, and can withstand harsh conditions. Panel Mount controllers, on the other hand, are designed to be mounted directly onto a control panel or equipment enclosure. They offer a more streamlined and integrated solution, fitting seamlessly into the existing equipment. Both types cater to specific installation requirements and provide reliable control and management capabilities at the edge of a network.</p></p>
