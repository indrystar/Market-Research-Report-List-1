<p><p>The market for robotic milking machines can be categorized into three types: standalone units, multiple stall units, and rotary units. Standalone units are designed for small-scale farms and consist of a single milking robot. Multiple stall units are suitable for medium-sized farms and consist of several milking robots, allowing multiple cows to be milked simultaneously. Rotary units are typically found in large-scale farms and involve a rotating platform where cows are milked as they move through the system. Each type caters to different farm sizes and milking capacities.</p></p>
