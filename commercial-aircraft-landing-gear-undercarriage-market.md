<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Commercial Aircraft Landing Gear and Undercarriage market types include Strut, Rocker, Framed, and Pontoon markets. In the Strut market, the landing gear is supported by a hydraulic cylinder and an oleo strut. The Rocker market involves a landing gear arrangement where the gear is hinged to a pivot. In the Framed market, the gear is enclosed within a frame structure for support. Lastly, the Pontoon market utilizes a floating structure attached to the belly of the aircraft to support the landing gear. These variations allow for different configurations suited to specific aircraft requirements.</p></p>
