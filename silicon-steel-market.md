<p><p>Silicon steel, also known as electrical steel, is a specific type of alloy used in the production of electrical equipment. Within the silicon steel market, there are two main types: oriented electrical steel and non-grain oriented electrical steel. Oriented electrical steel possesses a highly organized grain structure, making it ideal for use in transformers and generators. On the other hand, non-grain oriented electrical steel lacks a specific grain orientation, making it suitable for applications like motors and small transformers. Both types play a crucial role in the functioning of electrical equipment.</p></p>
