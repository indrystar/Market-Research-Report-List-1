The projection ratio refers to the distance between the projector and the screen compared to the width of the projected image. Lenses with a smaller projection ratio, such as below 0.38, are ideal for short-throw projectors, while higher ratios like above 2.1 are suitable for long-throw projectors. Different market segments cater to specific projection needs, allowing consumers to choose the lens type that best suits their requirements.</p></p>
