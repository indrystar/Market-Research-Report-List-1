<p><strong>The 3D Sensing Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereoscopic Vision</li><li>Structured Light Pattern</li><li>Time of Flight</li><li>Ultrasound</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Sensing Technology market is divided into several types. Stereoscopic Vision uses two cameras to capture images from slightly different angles, enabling depth perception. Structured Light Pattern emits patterns onto an object and calculates depth based on its deformation. Time of Flight measures the time it takes for light to travel and reflect back to determine distance. Ultrasound sends sound waves and measures their echo to create a 3D image. Finally, Others include technologies like laser triangulation and photogrammetry that use different techniques to capture and analyze depth information.</p></p>
