<p><p>The boring tools market consists of various types of tools used in drilling operations. Drill bits are cutting tools that create holes. Drilling tubulars are used as structural support during drilling processes. Drilling motors provide the necessary power to rotate the drill bit. Drill reamers and stabilizers expand and stabilize the hole. Drill collars add weight and stiffness to the drill string. Drill jars help release stuck drill strings. Drill swivels facilitate the rotation of the drill string. Mechanical thrusters provide thrust to the drill string.</p></p>
