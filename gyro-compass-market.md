<p><p>The gyro compass market comprises two main types: fiber optic gyro compasses and mechanical gyro compasses. Fiber optic gyro compasses use the concept of the Sagnac effect to measure the rotation of the Earth and determine true north. They offer higher accuracy, are immune to magnetic interference and have a longer lifespan, making them suitable for advanced applications. On the other hand, mechanical gyro compasses rely on mechanical components and require regular maintenance. Though less accurate and prone to errors due to magnetic disturbances, they are still widely used in various navigational systems.</p></p>
