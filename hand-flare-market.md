<p><p>Hand flares are classified into two main market types, namely distress flares and smoke signal flares. Distress flares are designed to be used in emergency situations, to signal for help and indicate distress. These flares typically emit bright, intense light to attract attention and often have a distinct color, such as red or orange. On the other hand, smoke signal flares produce dense smoke, which aids in visual signaling during emergencies, such as distress at sea or in remote areas. Both types of hand flares serve as essential safety tools to communicate distress and seek assistance.</p></p>
