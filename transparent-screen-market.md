<p><p>The transparent screen market consists of three main types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD screens use liquid crystals to create images and have been widely used in various industries for their cost-effectiveness and energy efficiency. LED screens use light-emitting diodes to produce images, offering high brightness and contrast. OLED screens use organic compounds to emit light, providing superior color reproduction and flexibility. These three types have their own distinct advantages and applications, and are commonly utilized in areas such as automotive displays, retail advertising, and architecture.</p></p>
