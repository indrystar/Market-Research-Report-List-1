<p><p>Auger Boring Machines (ABMs) are utilized in the construction industry for trenchless underground installations. There are two main types of ABMs: Soft Ground ABMs and Hard Rock ABMs. Soft Ground ABMs are designed specifically to work in soil conditions that are not suitable for traditional excavation methods. They use rotating augers to remove soil and create tunnels for various utility installations. On the other hand, Hard Rock ABMs are used for drilling through solid rock formations and rely on the application of substantial force to break and remove the rock during the boring process.</p></p>
