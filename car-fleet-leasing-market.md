<p><strong>The Car Fleet Leasing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Close End Lease</li><li>Open End Lease</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Close End Lease: In this type of car fleet leasing, the lessee returns the vehicle to the lessor at the end of the lease term without any further obligations. The responsibility for the vehicle's future value lies with the lessor, minimizing the lessee's risk. This type of lease is more popular for individuals and small businesses.</p><p>Open End Lease: In contrast, an open end lease transfers the risk of the vehicle's future value to the lessee. At the end of the lease term, the lessee is responsible for the vehicle's resale value. This type of lease is more common in commercial fleet leasing, where the lessee can benefit from the vehicle's increased value and potentially reduce their monthly payments.</p></p>
