<p><strong>The Arc Welding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arc welding equipment market can be categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. </p><p>In manual arc welding, the operator manually controls the welding process by adjusting the speed, angle, and position of the electrode. It requires skilled labor and is suitable for small-scale operations.</p><p>Semi-automatic arc welding involves a combination of manual and automated processes. The operator guides the electrode while the equipment automatically controls the welding current. It is ideal for medium-scale operations.</p><p>Fully automatic arc welding is a highly mechanized process where the equipment performs the welding operation without human intervention. It is suitable for large-scale industrial operations that require high production rates and consistent weld quality.</p></p>
