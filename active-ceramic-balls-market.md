<p><p>Active Ceramic Balls Market includes two main types: Filled Ceramic Balls and Milled Ceramic Balls. Filled Ceramic Balls are filled with different materials such as zeolite, tourmaline, or far infrared material, which release negative ions and improve water quality in various applications like water treatment and air purification. Milled Ceramic Balls, on the other hand, are finely milled ceramic particles that are used in industries like electronics, automotive, and aerospace for their high strength, thermal conductivity, and corrosion resistance properties. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing various processes and products.</p></p>
