<p><strong>The Conductivity Level Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Electrode</li><li>Suspended Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Conductivity Level Switch market consists of two main types: solid electrode and suspended electrode. Solid electrode level switches use a metallic probe inserted into the liquid to measure its conductivity. These switches are suitable for both conductive and non-conductive liquids. On the other hand, suspended electrode level switches measure conductivity by suspending an electrode in the liquid. These switches are commonly used for conductive liquids and offer advantages such as accurate sensing and easy installation. Both types of switches play a crucial role in monitoring and controlling liquid levels in various industries.</p></p>
