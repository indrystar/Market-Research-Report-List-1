<p><ul><li>Machine Stretch Film</li><li>Manual Stretch Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stretch film packaging is used to bundle and secure items during transportation or storage. There are two main types of stretch film packaging: machine stretch film and manual stretch film. Machine stretch film is typically used with automated wrapping equipment and is often thicker, providing higher levels of load stabilization. On the other hand, manual stretch film is thinner and generally applied by hand. It is commonly used for smaller or irregularly shaped items where automation is not feasible. Both types offer benefits such as protection from dust, moisture, and tampering.</p></p>
