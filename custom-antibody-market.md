<p><p>The custom antibody market consists of various types, including monoclonal, polyclonal, and others. Monoclonal antibodies are produced from cloned identical immune cells, ensuring high specificity and uniformity. Polyclonal antibodies are made from different immune cells, offering a broader range but lower specificity. Other types may include recombinant antibodies or bispecific antibodies, with unique properties and applications. These custom antibodies are designed and produced according to specific requirements, providing researchers and pharmaceutical companies with valuable tools for diagnostics, therapeutics, and research purposes.</p></p>
