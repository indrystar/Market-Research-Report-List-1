CMOS sensors play a crucial role in enabling features such as lane departure warning, adaptive cruise control, and automatic emergency braking, among others.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in CMOS sensor technology have led to improved image quality, enhanced low-light performance, and higher resolution. <p><strong>The CMOS Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS</li><li>Active Pixel Sensor CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS sensor market comprises two main types: Passive Pixel Sensor CMOS (PPS CMOS) and Active Pixel Sensor CMOS (APS CMOS). PPS CMOS sensors convert incoming light into an electrical charge which is then measured. They are commonly used in low-end applications due to their simple structure and low cost. APS CMOS sensors, on the other hand, not only measure the light but also amplify the signal, resulting in better image quality and higher performance. They are widely used in high-end applications such as professional photography and scientific imaging.</p></p>
