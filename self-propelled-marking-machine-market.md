<p><p>The self-propelled marking machine market consists of two main types: self-propelled thermoplastic road marking machines and self-propelled cold paint road marking machines. A self-propelled thermoplastic road marking machine applies thermoplastic materials on roads using heat to melt the material before applying it. On the other hand, a self-propelled cold paint road marking machine uses cold paint materials for road marking without the need for heat. Both types of machines are used in the road marking industry to ensure clear and durable road markings.</p></p>
