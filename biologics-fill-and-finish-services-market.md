<p><p>Biologics fill and finish services refer to the final stages of the manufacturing process for biologic drugs. The market includes two main types: liquid and lyophilized. Liquid biologics are in a ready-to-use form, requiring minimal preparation before administration. Lyophilized biologics undergo freeze-drying, converting them into a solid powder for improved stability and longer shelf life. These lyophilized drugs need reconstitution with a suitable liquid before use. The liquid market offers convenience and rapid administration, while the lyophilized market provides benefits like enhanced stability and easier storage.</p></p>
