<p><strong>The Full Body Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Scanner</li><li>Millimeter Wave Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full Body Scanners are divided into two main market types: X-ray Scanners and Millimeter Wave Scanners. X-ray Scanners use low-energy X-rays to create an image of the body's interior, detecting objects hidden beneath clothing. These scanners are commonly used at airports for security screening. On the other hand, Millimeter Wave Scanners emit non-ionizing electromagnetic waves to generate a three-dimensional image of the body's surface, highlighting any concealed items. These scanners are also used for security checks, offering an alternative to X-ray Scanners and addressing privacy concerns.</p></p>
