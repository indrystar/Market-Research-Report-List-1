<p><strong>The Aircraft Simulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cockpit Type</li><li>PC-Based Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft simulators are divided into two main market types: Cockpit Type and PC-Based Type. Cockpit Type simulators are physical replicas of aircraft cockpits, providing a realistic training environment for pilots. They simulate the aircraft's controls, displays, and systems, offering an immersive experience. On the other hand, PC-Based Type simulators are software-based and run on personal computers. They offer cost-effective solutions for pilot training as they can be easily accessed and used anywhere with a computer. While Cockpit Type simulators excel in realism, PC-Based Type simulators focus on accessibility and affordability.</p></p>
