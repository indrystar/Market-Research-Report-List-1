<p><p>The non-woven construction fabrics market includes three main types: PP (polypropylene), PET (polyethylene terephthalate), and PE (polyethylene). PP is a widely used fabric, known for its high strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals and moisture. PET fabric offers excellent dimensional stability and good resistance to abrasion and tearing, making it suitable for applications that require high tensile strength. PE fabric, on the other hand, is lightweight and offers good flexibility and a high resistance to impact, making it ideal for applications that require easy handling and transportation.</p></p>
