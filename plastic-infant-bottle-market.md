<p><p>A plastic infant bottle is a bottle made of plastic material that is specifically designed for feeding infants. It is commonly used to provide formula milk or breast milk to babies. The growth can be attributed to several factors. Plastic infant bottles provide a practical solution for busy parents as they are lightweight, durable, and easy to clean.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in bottle design and manufacturing technology have led to the development of innovative features in plastic infant bottles. For instance, some bottles now come with anti-colic systems that reduce the intake of air during feeding, minimizing the risk of discomfort for the baby. Additionally, there has been a growing preference for BPA-free bottles, as concerns over the potential health risks associated with BPA (bisphenol A) have emerged. This has led to the introduction of BPA-free plastic materials in infant bottles, which has further fueled market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the rise in disposable income in emerging economies, coupled with increasing urbanization, has contributed to the growth of the plastic infant bottle market.
