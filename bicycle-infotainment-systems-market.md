<p><p>Bicycle Infotainment Systems refer to the technology used to enhance the cycling experience. Wearable Kits are a type of system that can be worn by the rider, typically in the form of a smartwatch or a head-up display attached to the helmet. These kits provide information such as speed, distance, navigation, and even heart rate monitoring. On the other hand, On-Board Kits are integrated directly into the bicycle frame or handlebars. They offer similar features as wearable kits but are permanently attached to the bike, providing a seamless cycling experience without the need for additional accessories.</p></p>
