<p><p>The Cervical Spine Immobilizer Market is categorized into two main types: soft collar and rigid collar. Soft collars are made of foam material and provide minimal support to the neck. They are commonly used for mild neck injuries or as a preventive measure. On the other hand, rigid collars are made of hard plastic and provide more stability and immobilization to the cervical spine. They are generally used for severe neck injuries or after cervical spine surgery. Both types of collars play a crucial role in supporting and immobilizing the neck to promote healing and prevent further damage.</p></p>
