<p><p>SiC power modules refer to power electronic devices that utilize silicon carbide (SiC) as the semiconductor material. There are two main types of SiC power modules in the market: hybrid SiC modules and full SiC modules. Hybrid SiC modules combine SiC devices with traditional silicon-based components, offering improved performance and efficiency compared to conventional modules. Full SiC modules, on the other hand, solely use SiC devices, providing even higher performance, reduced losses, and higher operating temperatures. Both types of SiC power modules are gaining popularity in various industries due to their enhanced power handling capabilities.</p></p>
