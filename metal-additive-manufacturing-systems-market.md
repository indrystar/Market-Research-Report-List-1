<p><strong>The Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Bed Fusion</li><li>Directed Energy Deposition</li><li>Binder Jetting</li><li>Sheet Lamination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems market includes various types of technologies used for producing metal parts. Powder Bed Fusion involves the melting and fusing of metal powders layer by layer to create the final product. Directed Energy Deposition uses focused energy beams to melt metal feedstock and build up the part. Binder Jetting combines powdered metal and a binding agent that is selectively deposited and solidified. Sheet Lamination involves stacking layers of metal sheets and shaping them with CNC machining or welding. These technologies offer different advantages and capabilities for metal additive manufacturing.</p></p>
