<p><p>The Optical Fiber Distribution Box Market offers various types of optical splitters, including 1:4, 1:8, 1:16, and 1:32. These splitters are used to divide the high-speed optical signals coming from a single optical fiber into multiple streams, allowing for efficient distribution to several end users. The 1:4 splitter divides the signal into four streams, the 1:8 splitter into eight streams, the 1:16 splitter into sixteen streams, and the 1:32 splitter into thirty-two streams. This diverse range of optical splitters caters to different network requirements and enables effective optical signal distribution.</p></p>
