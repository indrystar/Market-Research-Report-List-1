<p><p>The Electrical Safety Barriers Market offers three types of barriers: 1-Channel, 2-Channel, and Multi-Channel. 1-Channel barriers are designed to prevent electrical leakage and ensure safety in single circuits. 2-Channel barriers offer protection for two circuits separately, reducing the risk of simultaneous faults. Multi-Channel barriers provide safety measures for multiple circuits, providing enhanced protection in complex electrical systems. These different barriers cater to a variety of electrical setups, offering users flexibility and customization in safeguarding against electrical hazards.</p></p>
