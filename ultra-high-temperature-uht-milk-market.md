<p><p>Ultra High Temperature (UHT) milk, also known as long-life milk, is a type of milk that has undergone heat treatment at a temperature above 135 degrees Celsius for a short duration, typically 2-5 seconds. This process extends the shelf life of milk for up to 6-9 months without the need for refrigeration.</p><p>The UHT milk market is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the convenience factor is driving its popularity, as UHT milk can be stored at room temperature, eliminating the need for refrigeration. This makes it ideal for consumers in regions with limited access to refrigeration facilities. Additionally, UHT milk offers greater flexibility in terms of transportation and distribution, as it can be shipped over long distances without the need for cold chains.</p><p>The future outlook of the UHT milk market is promising, with a projected CAGR of 7.2% during the forecast period. These regions have a growing population and rising urbanization rates, which create a favorable environment for the expansion of UHT milk consumption.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a rising demand for organic and plant-based UHT milk products.
