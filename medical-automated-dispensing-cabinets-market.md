<p><ul><li>Tablet ADCs</li><li>Dose ADCs</li><li>Vial ADCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical automated dispensing cabinets (ADCs) come in various types: Tablet ADCs, Dose ADCs, and Vial ADCs. Tablet ADCs are designed to dispense unit-dose medications in solid tablet or capsule forms. Dose ADCs, on the other hand, are specifically designed to dispense liquid medications in specific doses. Lastly, Vial ADCs are used to store and dispense medication in vials, accommodating a larger variety of medications and doses. These different types of ADCs provide efficient and accurate dispensing of medications in healthcare settings, improving patient safety and workflow.</p></p>
