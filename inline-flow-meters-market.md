<p><p>Inline flow meters are devices used to measure the flow rate of fluids, gases, or vapors within a pipe or tubing. There are two main types of inline flow meters: digital flow meters and mechanical flow meters. Digital flow meters utilize electronic sensors and display the flow rate digitally, providing accurate and real-time measurements. Mechanical flow meters, on the other hand, use mechanical mechanisms such as rotating vanes or turbines to measure the flow rate and display it through analog dials or indicators. Both types of flow meters play a crucial role in various industries for monitoring and controlling fluid flow.</p></p>
