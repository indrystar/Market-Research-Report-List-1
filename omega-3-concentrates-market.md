<p><p>Omega-3 concentrates refer to highly concentrated forms of omega-3 fatty acids used for various health benefits. There are three main types of omega-3 concentrates: triglyceride, ethyl ester, and phospholipids. Triglyceride concentrates are extracted from fish oil and are the closest to natural omega-3 in our diet. Ethyl ester concentrates are produced by manipulating triglycerides for higher concentration. Phospholipid concentrates are derived from krill oil and contain omega-3 in a form easily absorbed by the body. Each type of concentrate offers different advantages and is used in various dietary supplements and functional foods.</p></p>
