<p><p>Elbow replacement refers to the artificial replacement of damaged elbow joints. There are two types of elbow replacement markets: partial elbow replacement and total elbow replacement. Partial elbow replacement involves replacing only a portion of the damaged joint, typically the head of the radius bone. Total elbow replacement, on the other hand, involves replacing the entire elbow joint with an artificial implant. These procedures are typically performed to alleviate pain, improve joint mobility, and restore function in individuals with severe elbow joint damage or disorders.</p></p>
