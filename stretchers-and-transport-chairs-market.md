<p><p>Stretchers and transport chairs are medical equipment designed to safely transport patients within a healthcare facility. Stretchers typically have a reclining backrest and can be adjusted to various positions to accommodate the patient's needs during transportation. They are commonly used in hospitals, nursing homes, and ambulances. Transport chairs, on the other hand, are lightweight and portable devices used for short-distance patient transfers, such as moving a patient from a bed to a wheelchair.</p><p>The stretchers and transport chairs market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. <p><p>The stretchers and transport chairs market can be classified into three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual stretchers and transport chairs require physical force to operate, with healthcare professionals or caregivers manually pushing or pulling them as needed. Electric stretchers and transport chairs are designed to be operated using an electric power source, allowing for easier movement and reduced strain on the users. Hydraulic stretchers and transport chairs use hydraulic mechanisms to control height adjustments and movements, ensuring smooth and controlled transfers of patients. These different types cater to various needs and preferences in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
