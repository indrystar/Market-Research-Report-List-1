<p><p>The household hair dryer market consists of two main types: centrifugal hair dryers and axial hair dryers. Centrifugal hair dryers use a motor-driven fan that draws air into the unit and then blows it out in a concentrated stream, providing powerful and quick drying. On the other hand, axial hair dryers use a propeller-like blade to create airflow, which is then directed through the heating element for drying. Both types are widely available in the market and offer varying features and price ranges to cater to the diverse needs of consumers.</p></p>
