<p><p>The multi-layer security market can be categorized into proactive, detective, and reactive types. Proactive security focuses on preventing attacks and unauthorized access by implementing measures such as firewalls and antivirus software. Detective security involves monitoring and detecting potential threats or breaches through techniques like intrusion detection systems and security audits. Reactive security aims to respond to security incidents or breaches and minimize their impact through incident response plans and data recovery procedures. These three types work together to provide comprehensive protection and ensure the resilience of an organization's security infrastructure.</p></p>
