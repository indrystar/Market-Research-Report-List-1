The integration of advanced features like internet connectivity, voice recognition, and content streaming capabilities in televisions is attracting consumers, which, in turn, is propelling the market growth.</p><p>However, the market also faces some challenges. The high cost of plasma display modules compared to other display technologies, such as LCD and OLED, can hinder their widespread adoption, particularly in price-sensitive markets.
