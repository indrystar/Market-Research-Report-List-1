<p><p>Automatic production lines can be categorized into two main types: intelligent and unintelligent. These systems can adapt to changing conditions, optimize processes, and identify and resolve issues autonomously. On the other hand, unintelligent production lines are more basic, relying primarily on automated systems to perform predefined tasks without the ability to learn or adapt. They operate based on fixed instructions and are suitable for repetitive and predictable production processes.</p></p>
