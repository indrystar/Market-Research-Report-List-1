<p><p>Multi-turn electric actuators are commonly used in the industrial market for various applications. There are two market types for these actuators: intrusive and non-invasive. Intrusive actuators require mechanical connections to the valve stem or shaft, allowing them to directly control the valve's position. On the other hand, non-invasive actuators do not require any physical connections and instead use non-contact technologies such as magnetic, hydraulic, or pneumatic principles to control the valve. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
