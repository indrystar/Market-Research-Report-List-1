<p><p>Mass transfer equipment is widely used in various industrial processes for transferring materials between gas and liquid phases. There are different types of mass transfer equipment available in the market. Column internals refer to components inside a column that enhance mass transfer efficiency. Random packing consists of various small particles randomly placed inside a column to increase surface area for mass transfer. Structured packing comprises of specifically designed sheets or blocks that provide an organized surface for efficient mass transfer. Trays are horizontal or vertical platforms inside a column that promote mass transfer through contact between phases.</p></p>
