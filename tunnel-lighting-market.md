<p><p>The tunnel lighting market encompasses various types of lighting technologies. LED lighting, known for its energy efficiency and long lifespan, is widely preferred. Electrodeless lighting, which uses electromagnetic induction, is notable for its durability. Sodium lighting uses sodium vapor to generate light and is known for its high luminous efficacy. Incandescent lighting, known for its warm glow, is being phased out due to its low efficiency. Fluorescent lighting, which relies on the excitation of mercury vapor, is commonly used due to its brightness and efficiency. There are also other types of tunnel lighting technologies available in the market.</p></p>
