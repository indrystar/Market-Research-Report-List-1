<p><ul><li>Pretreatment</li><li>Cell Assembly</li><li>Post Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithium battery manufacturing machinery market can be categorized into three main types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post processing. Pretreatment machinery includes equipment used for materials preparation such as coating and drying. Cell assembly machinery involves the assembly of individual battery cells into a complete battery pack. Post processing machinery includes equipment for testing, inspecting, and packaging finished battery packs. Each type of machinery plays a vital role in the production process of lithium batteries, ensuring efficiency and quality control.</p></p>
