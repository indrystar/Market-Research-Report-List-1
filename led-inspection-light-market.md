<p><strong>The LED Inspection Light Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic</li><li>No Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED Inspection Light market can be classified into two types: Magnetic and No Magnetic. Magnetic inspection lights are equipped with adhesion capabilities, allowing them to securely attach to metallic surfaces. This feature provides convenience and flexibility during inspections, as the lights can be placed in various positions. On the other hand, No Magnetic inspection lights lack magnetic properties and are not intended for use on metallic surfaces. These lights are suitable for inspections that require non-magnetic environments or when attaching to other surfaces using alternative methods.</p></p>
