<p><p>The wheelchair medical equipment market offers two main types: electric and manual wheelchairs. Electric wheelchairs are powered by a battery and offer more convenience as they can be easily controlled with a joystick or remote control. They are suitable for individuals with limited mobility or strength. On the other hand, manual wheelchairs rely on human power and require someone to push them. These wheelchairs are lighter, more affordable, and can be easily transported. They are usually used by individuals who have enough upper body strength to operate them. Both types serve different needs and preferences within the wheelchair market.</p></p>
