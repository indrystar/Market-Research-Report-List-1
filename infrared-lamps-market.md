<p><strong>The Infrared Lamps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near Infrared</li><li>Medium Infrared</li><li>Far Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared lamps are categorized into three types based on their wavelength and energy output: Near Infrared, Medium Infrared, and Far Infrared. Near Infrared lamps emit shorter wavelengths and are primarily used in medical treatments, pain relief, and industrial processes. Medium Infrared lamps have longer wavelengths and are commonly used in heating applications, food processing, and drying processes. Far Infrared lamps emit the longest wavelengths and are mainly used in saunas, therapy devices, and heating systems. These different types of infrared lamps cater to various industries and applications based on their specific wavelength characteristics.</p></p>
