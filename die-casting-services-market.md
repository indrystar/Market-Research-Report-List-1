<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Gravity</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die casting services market consists of three types: high pressure, gravity, and low pressure casting. High pressure die casting involves injecting molten metal at high speeds and pressures into a metal die, allowing for the fast production of complex and detailed parts. Gravity die casting utilizes gravity itself to fill the die with molten metal, resulting in less shrinkage and high-quality surface finish. Low pressure die casting involves applying low air pressure to force molten metal into the die, producing high integrity parts with reduced porosity.</p></p>
