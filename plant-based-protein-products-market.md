<p><p>Plant-based protein products are classified into two main types: complete plant protein and incomplete plant protein. Complete plant protein products contain all nine essential amino acids that the body needs, making them a high-quality source of protein. In contrast, incomplete plant protein products lack one or more essential amino acids and may need to be combined with other plant-based protein sources to ensure a balanced intake of all essential amino acids. Both types offer sustainable and environmentally friendly alternatives to animal-based protein products.</p></p>
