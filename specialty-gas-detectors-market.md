<p><p>Specialty gas detectors are devices used to monitor and detect the presence of harmful gases in various applications. The market for specialty gas detectors can be classified into two types - fixed and portable. Fixed gas detectors are permanently installed in a specific location to continuously monitor gas levels and provide alarms or notifications when gas concentrations exceed pre-set limits. Portable gas detectors, on the other hand, are compact and handheld devices that allow for mobility, enabling users to carry out gas detection in different areas or during fieldwork.</p></p>
