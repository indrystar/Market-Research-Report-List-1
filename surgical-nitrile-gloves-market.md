<p><p>Surgical Nitrile Gloves are disposable gloves made from synthetic rubber known as nitrile. These gloves are primarily used in healthcare settings during surgeries and other medical procedures to protect both the healthcare personnel and patients from cross-contamination and infections. Nitrile gloves offer several advantages over traditional latex gloves, including higher puncture resistance, enhanced chemical resistance, and improved sensitivity.</p><p>The Surgical Nitrile Gloves market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing awareness about infection control measures, rising incidence of healthcare-associated infections, and the growing demand for disposable gloves in the healthcare industry. Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has further propelled the demand for surgical gloves worldwide, as healthcare systems strive to prevent the spread of the virus through strict infection control protocols.</p><p>Various trends are shaping the Surgical Nitrile Gloves market. One of the key trends is the shift towards powder-free nitrile gloves, as powdered gloves have been linked to allergies and skin irritation. Manufacturers are focusing on developing advanced and innovative products to cater to the specific needs of healthcare professionals, such as textured gloves for better grip and precision. Moreover, sustainable and eco-friendly glove options are gaining traction in the market as the healthcare industry becomes more environmentally conscious.</p><p>The Surgical Nitrile Gloves Market is expected to witness a steady growth rate during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, growing geriatric population, and the rise in the number of surgeries globally are anticipated to drive the market growth. Additionally, the implementation of strict regulations regarding infection control and the adoption of safety measures by healthcare facilities will further boost the demand for surgical nitrile gloves.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Powdered Gloves</li><li>Non-Powdered Gloves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical nitrile gloves market can be categorized into two types: powdered gloves and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a fine powder, typically cornstarch, to facilitate easier wearing and removal. They are commonly used in medical procedures, such as surgeries. On the other hand, non-powdered gloves do not have any external coating and are preferred in industries where powder can contaminate the working environment. Both types fulfill the need for protection and hygiene during surgical procedures, but those with specific concerns about powder may opt for non-powdered gloves.</p></p>
