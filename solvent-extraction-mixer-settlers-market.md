<p><p>Solvent Extraction Mixer-Settlers are widely used in various industries for liquid-liquid separation processes. The market for these mixers can be classified into two types: stand-alone type and in-column type. Stand-alone type mixers are independent units, designed with separate vessels for mixing and settling. They are suitable for small-scale applications and offer flexibility in operation. On the other hand, in-column type mixers are integrated into a column, where mixing and settling occur within the same unit. These mixers are ideal for large-scale operations and provide higher efficiency due to minimized piping and improved mass transfer.</p></p>
