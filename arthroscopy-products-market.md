<p><p>The arthroscopy products market is categorized into various types. Arthroscopes are devices used for visualizing and diagnosing joint conditions. Arthroscopic fluid management systems help maintain clear vision during arthroscopic surgeries. Arthroscopic implants are used for joint repair or reconstruction. Arthroscopy radiofrequency systems use radio waves for tissue ablation or coagulation. Fixation devices are used for stabilizing fractures and ligament repairs.
