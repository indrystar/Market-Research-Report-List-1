<p><p>Hand pump market offers various types such as rotary pumps, lever action pumps, piston pumps, drum siphon pumps, and hydraulic hand pumps. Rotary pumps use rotating motion to transfer fluids, while lever action pumps involve pumping by pushing and pulling a lever. Piston pumps use reciprocating motion to transfer liquids, and drum siphon pumps are designed to extract fluids from drums or barrels. Hydraulic hand pumps utilize hydraulic force to transfer liquids. These different pump types cater to various industries and applications, providing efficient, manual fluid transfer solutions.</p></p>
