<p><p>The motorized pulley market is segmented into three types: air-cooled motorized pulley, oil-cooled motorized pulley, and oil-immersed motorized pulley. </p><p>An air-cooled motorized pulley utilizes air as a cooling mechanism to prevent overheating during operation. It is suitable for environments with low ambient temperatures and minimal contaminants. </p><p>An oil-cooled motorized pulley relies on oil circulation to dissipate heat and maintain optimal performance. It is preferred for applications where higher cooling efficiency is required. </p><p>An oil-immersed motorized pulley is submerged in oil, which serves as both a cooling and lubrication medium. It offers enhanced cooling capabilities and is often used in harsh operating conditions.</p></p>
