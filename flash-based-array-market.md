<p><p>A flash-based array, also known as an all-flash array (AFA), is a type of storage system that uses only flash memory to store and retrieve data. The increasing adoption of flash-based arrays in data centers and cloud environments, owing to their high-performance capabilities, is a major factor driving the market growth. The need for faster data access and retrieval, as well as efficient data management, enhances the demand for flash-based arrays. The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms into flash-based arrays enables intelligent data management and predictive analytics capabilities. The emergence of software-defined storage (SDS) and hyper-converged infrastructure (HCI) solutions incorporating flash-based arrays is also gaining traction in the market.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing focus on data security and data protection measures.
