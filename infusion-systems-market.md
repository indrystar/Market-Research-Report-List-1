<p><p>Infusion systems are medical devices used to deliver fluids, such as medications or nutrients, into a patient's body in a controlled manner. The market for infusion systems can be categorized into different types. General purpose or volumetric pumps are versatile devices that deliver fluids at a regulated rate. Patient Controlled Analgesia pumps allow patients to self-administer pain medication within predetermined limits. Ambulatory pumps are portable and designed for continuous infusion during patient mobility. Others include specialty pumps for specific therapies or innovative technologies that enhance infusion delivery.</p></p>
