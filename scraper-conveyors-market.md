<p><p>Scraper conveyors are used to transport bulk materials in a range of industries. Vertical scraper conveyors are designed for upward movement, such as lifting materials from underground mines. They are equipped with multiple blades that rotate vertically, allowing materials to be carried to higher levels. Horizontal scraper conveyors, on the other hand, transport materials in a horizontal direction. They typically consist of a long trough with multiple interconnected blades, which push the materials forward. Both types of conveyors play crucial roles in efficiently moving materials across various industrial processes.</p></p>
