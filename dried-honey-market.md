<p><strong>The Dried Honey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dried honey market consists of two main types: granular and powder. Granular dried honey is made by dehydrating liquid honey and breaking it into small granules. It retains the natural texture and sweetness of honey, making it ideal for toppings and mix-ins. Powdered dried honey is finely ground into a powder form, making it easier to mix and dissolve in recipes and beverages. It offers the same sweetness and flavor as granular honey but with a more convenient and versatile application. Both granular and powder dried honey provide a convenient way to enjoy the taste of honey in various culinary creations.</p></p>
