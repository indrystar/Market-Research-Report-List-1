<p><p>The healthcare virtual assistant market comprises two main types: chatbot and smart speaker. Chatbots are text-based virtual assistants that employ artificial intelligence to interact and assist users in healthcare-related tasks, such as scheduling appointments or providing basic medical information. On the other hand, smart speakers are voice-activated virtual assistants that use voice recognition technology to respond to user inquiries or perform healthcare-related functions, like medication reminders or monitoring vital signs. Both types aim to enhance healthcare accessibility and convenience while integrating technology seamlessly into patients' lives.</p></p>
