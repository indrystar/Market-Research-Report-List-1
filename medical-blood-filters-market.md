<p><strong>The Medical Blood Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Blood Transfusion</li><li>Platelet Transfusion</li><li>Red Cell Transfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical blood filters are used in various types of blood transfusions. Whole blood transfusion filters are designed to remove impurities and debris from donated blood before it is transfused to patients. Platelet transfusion filters are used to separate platelets from whole blood, ensuring that only platelets are transfused. Red cell transfusion filters are specifically designed to remove red blood cells from whole blood, allowing for the isolation and transfusion of red cells alone. These filters help ensure safe and efficient transfusion procedures by removing unwanted elements from the blood.</p></p>
