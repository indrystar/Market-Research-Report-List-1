<p><p>Metal braces are orthodontic devices used to align and straighten teeth. They consist of brackets, wires, and bands made from high-quality stainless steel or titanium. These components work together to apply gentle pressure on the teeth, gradually moving them into the desired position.</p><p>The metal braces market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing prevalence of dental malocclusions and a growing emphasis on aesthetics. The demand for metal braces is driven by factors such as rising awareness about oral health and the availability of advanced orthodontic treatments.</p><p>One of the key trends in the metal braces market is the introduction of self-ligating metal braces. These braces eliminate the need for elastic or metal ties, making the treatment more comfortable and efficient. Self-ligating metal braces also offer faster treatment times and require fewer adjustments, making them a preferable choice for both patients and orthodontists.</p><p>Another trend driving the market growth is the rising popularity of esthetic metal braces. These braces use clear or tooth-colored brackets and wires, providing a more discreet treatment option for individuals who are conscious about their appearance. Esthetic metal braces offer the benefits of traditional metal braces while reducing their visual impact.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in orthodontic technology, such as computer-aided design and 3D printing, have improved the precision and efficiency of metal braces. These advancements have led to increased adoption of metal braces among orthodontists and improved patient experience during treatment.</p><p>In summary, the metal braces market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period.
