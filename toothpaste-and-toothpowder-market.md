Silica type toothpaste/powder contains abrasive silica particles that help in removing stains and plaque. Calcium carbonate type products use this compound as a mild abrasive. Phosphate type toothpaste/powder contains phosphate salts that prevent the formation of tartar. Aluminum hydroxide type products use aluminum hydroxide as an abrasive and are often recommended for sensitive teeth.
