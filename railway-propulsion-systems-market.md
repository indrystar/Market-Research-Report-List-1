<p><p>Railway propulsion systems market is divided into two main types - Diesel Propulsion Systems and Electric Locomotive Propulsion Systems. Diesel propulsion systems use diesel engines to power trains, providing flexibility in remote areas without electrified tracks. On the other hand, electric locomotive propulsion systems use electricity from overhead lines or third rails to power trains, offering cleaner and more sustainable operation. Both types of propulsion systems play a crucial role in the railway industry, catering to different needs and operating conditions.</p></p>
