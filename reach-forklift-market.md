<p><p>Reach forklifts come in two main types, namely Stand-Up Reach Forklifts and Sit-Down Reach Forklifts. Stand-Up Reach Forklifts are designed for operators to stand while operating the forklift, allowing for greater visibility and maneuverability in tight spaces. On the other hand, Sit-Down Reach Forklifts are equipped with a seated cab for the operator, providing a more comfortable driving experience for long shifts. Both types of reach forklifts are popular in warehouses and distribution centers for their efficiency in reaching high storage shelves.</p></p>
