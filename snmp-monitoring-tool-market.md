<p><p>SNMP, or Simple Network Management Protocol, is a widely used protocol for managing and monitoring network devices. The SNMP monitoring tool market consists of several types. SNMP Manager refers to the software or device used to collect and interpret data from managed devices. Managed devices are the network devices, such as routers or switches, that are being monitored. SNMP agents reside on these devices, collecting and forwarding data to the manager. The Management Information Base (MIB) market pertains to the database of variables and parameters that the SNMP manager uses to understand and manage the network devices.</p></p>
