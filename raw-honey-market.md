<p><strong>The Raw Honey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyfloral Honey</li><li>Monofloral Honey</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Raw honey is categorized into two main market types: polyfloral honey and monofloral honey. Polyfloral honey is derived from nectar collected by bees from multiple flower sources, resulting in a diverse and mixed flavor profile. It reflects the variety and abundance of flowers available in a particular region. On the other hand, monofloral honey is produced when bees collect nectar from predominantly one type of flower. This type of honey possesses distinct characteristics and flavors attributed to the dominant floral source. Both polyfloral and monofloral honey provide options for consumers seeking varied or specific honey tastes.</p></p>
