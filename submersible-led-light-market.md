<p><p>The submersible LED light market is comprised of two main types: stationary type and wall-mounted type. Stationary type submersible LED lights are designed to be placed on the bottom of a body of water such as a pool or pond, providing a fixed source of illumination. On the other hand, wall-mounted type submersible LED lights are installed on the walls of swimming pools or other water bodies, offering a more focused and adjustable lighting solution. Both types cater to the needs of individuals looking to enhance the visual appeal and ambiance of their aquatic environments.</p></p>
