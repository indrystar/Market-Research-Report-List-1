<p><p>Spin bikes are an essential component of fitness equipment, designed to simulate the experience of outdoor cycling. They come in two main market types - magnetic resistance and felt pad resistance. Magnetic resistance spin bikes use magnets to generate resistance, creating a quiet and smooth riding experience. On the other hand, felt pad resistance spin bikes use friction between a felt pad and the flywheel to create resistance. Both types offer varying levels of resistance to provide users with a challenging workout and allow for adjustments based on individual fitness levels and goals.</p></p>
