<p><p>Diaphragm pumps are classified into two main types: air-operated and electrically operated. Air-operated diaphragm pumps are powered by compressed air and are suitable for applications where electricity is not readily available or restricted. They are commonly used in industries such as mining, construction, and oil and gas. On the other hand, electrically operated diaphragm pumps are powered by electricity and are preferred in situations where a consistent power source is available. They are often used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and water treatment.</p></p>
